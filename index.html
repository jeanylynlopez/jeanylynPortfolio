About Me

Hi! My name’s Jeanylyn and I’m a web developer with a background in journalism.

I started my journey as a dev more than a year ago, taking part-time courses with HackerYou. Now, I’m just about to finish the college’s immersive nine-week bootcamp.

Before the program, I was a digital editor for PMNA under the Canadian Press, where I was responsible for editing and posting stories on the Globe and Mail and Toronto Star’s website. I’m no stranger to fast-paced environments.

When I’m not coding, you can find me watching a Raptors game, playing video games or exploring Toronto for great places to eat.
